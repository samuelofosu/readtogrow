About Read to Grow Club

At Read to Grow, we believe that readers are leaders. Our community empowers individuals to explore ideas, share perspectives, and develop the mindset needed to grow in knowledge, confidence, and character. Through consistent reading and engaging discussions, we build better thinkers and stronger leaders.

Our Mission

To inspire lifelong learning and leadership through reading, critical thinking, and meaningful conversations.

Our Vision

To nurture a global community of readers who grow together, share knowledge, and use wisdom to transform the world around them.

Club Rules

  • Respect every member's opinion and voice.
  • Be consistent — read assigned books and participate actively.
  • Encourage others and share insights, not criticism.
  • Keep discussions positive and focused on growth.
  • Punctuality for meetings and events is expected.
  • No offensive or discriminatory language or behavior.
  • Stay open-minded — every book has something to teach.
